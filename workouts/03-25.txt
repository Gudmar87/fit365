<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><br></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><strong><em>First step:</em></strong><em>&nbsp;Count 5-10 single unders and then try to do one double.&nbsp;Rest and repeat.</em></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><em>Second step: Count 5-10 single unders and then try to do&nbsp;one double and continue with single unders.&nbsp;Rest and repeat.</em></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><strong><em>Third step</em></strong><em>:&nbsp;Count 5-10 single unders and then try to do as many double unders you can in a row.&nbsp;Rest and repeat.</em></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><strong><em>Fourth step</em></strong><em>: Start with one single under and&nbsp;then try to do as many double unders you can in a row.&nbsp;Rest and repeat.</em></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><strong><em>Fifth step</em></strong><em>: Start directly with double unders and try to do as specifik amount of them,&nbsp;like 10, 20, 30. Rest and repeat.</em></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><br></div>
<div style="text-align: start;color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"><em>(Don&rsquo;t&nbsp;advance to fast through the steps. Master one before moving on).</em></div>
